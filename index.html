04

SOLUTIONS

Actionable strategies to reduce food delivery waste at every level.

🌿

Biodegradable Packaging

Switch to plant-based, compostable containers made from bagasse, cornstarch, or bamboo fibre. Already adopted by brands like Zomato's "Pure Veg" fleet.

Industry
🔄

Reusable Container Programs

Deposit-based reusable container systems where customers return packaging on next delivery. Pilots in Bengaluru have shown 40% waste reduction.

Innovation
📜

Extended Producer Responsibility

Mandate platforms to fund collection & recycling of packaging waste under India's EPR framework and the Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016.

Policy
🙋

Consumer Opt-Out for Cutlery

A simple checkbox: "I don't need cutlery." Zomato & Swiggy's opt-out feature has already prevented 2.2 billion+ pieces of cutlery waste.

Consumer
🏭

Waste-to-Energy Conversion

Non-recyclable packaging can be processed in waste-to-energy plants. India targets 100+ WtE plants by 2030 under the Swachh Bharat Mission.

Technology
📢

Awareness Campaigns

Educate consumers on proper waste segregation and the environmental impact of food delivery packaging through in-app nudges and social campaigns.

Education
